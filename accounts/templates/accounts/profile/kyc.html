Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KYC verification

What documents are required for KYC verification?

Required documents vary based on your user type:

  • Travel Agents: Trade License, SCTA License, CR Number
  • Service Suppliers: Company Registration, VAT Certificate
  • Individual Users: Passport or Saudi ID, Proof of Address
  • Hajj/Umrah Agents: Hajj License, SCTA License
How long does KYC verification take?

Standard verification takes 24-48 hours during business days. Complex cases may take up to 5 business days. You'll receive email notifications at each stage.

What if my documents are rejected?

If documents are rejected, you'll receive an email with specific reasons. You can reupload corrected documents. Common rejection reasons include:

  • Blurry or unreadable images
  • Expired documents
  • Name mismatch with account
  • Incomplete document pages
  • Unsupported file format
Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and comply with Saudi Arabian data protection regulations (PDPL). Your documents are:

  • Encrypted during transfer and storage
  • Accessible only to authorized compliance staff
  • Automatically deleted after verification (unless required by law)
  • Protected by strict access controls
Can I update my KYC documents later?

Yes, you can update expired documents or add new ones at any time. However, significant changes (like company ownership) may require re-verification. Document updates typically take 24 hours to process.
